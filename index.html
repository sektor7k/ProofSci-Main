What is Proof Sci ?

Proof Sci is a decentralized science and technology platform. Users can create profiles on the platform and generate project ideas. Projects can be in different fields, e.g., electrical/electronics, biotechnology, the environment, or medicine.

Users can take ownership of their projects by embedding them in an NFT. A project page is created for each NFT, and the owner can record the progress of the project on this page. Users can also open a donation area to support their projects. .

Proof Sci offers an innovative approach to supporting scientific and technological progress and enables the realization of innovative ideas in science and technology. .

PROOF NFT

Proof-NFT is a system that allows researchers to hold ownership of their creative ideas as NFTs on the blockchain. This way, researchers can protect the ownership rights of their ideas and immortalize them on the blockchain.

New Method for Quantum Computing

A new method for quantum computing has been developed. This method allows quantum computers to perform faster and more accurate calculations. The new method helps quantum bits to be better controlled and make fewer errors. This development could further advance quantum computing technology and enable larger and more complex calculations in the future.

A New Method for Electrolysis Developed

Electrolysis is a method in which electric current is used to carry out chemical reactions. Recently, researchers have developed a new method for electrolysis. This method could allow for more efficient and less energy-consuming electrolysis processes. The new method helps the electrodes to be better controlled and wear less. This development could allow electrolysis technology to advance further and enable more sustainable chemical production methods in the future.

Cell division is an important process for the growth and renewal of cells. Recently, researchers have discovered a new control mechanism for cell division. This mechanism helps cells to be better controlled and make fewer mistakes.
